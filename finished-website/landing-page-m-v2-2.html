About Us
Hello, we are a company seeking to make people productive through video games. There will be various games like racing,
boss fighting, map findings, and more!

Furthermore, we have different types of options within the app. We have quests, parties, clans, sessions, to do lists, friend tracker, and various other features!
Fight bosses with friends!
Level up your characters  and pets. Fight bosses with friends and complete challenges with your clan!
Group Sessions

Group sessions but you have the option to conduct group and indicifual sessions. During these sessions you can chat related to studies, execute tasks on the todo list, and even listen to music!

Marketplace

You can buy various characters in the market system for bragging rights and other amazing purposes, but in order to do so, you have to work up to that level...

Quests
Complete quests in order to achieve awards.
Clans
Work together with your friends in order to compete in video game challenges and get tasks done.
Challenges
Complete achievements and challenges in order to get more rewards.
